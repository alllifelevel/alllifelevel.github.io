Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 9, 2026

Welcome to NoKy Keyboard ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are deeply committed to protecting the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy details our practices regarding information collection, usage, and security for the NoKy Keyboard mobile application on Android.

🔒 Absolute Privacy Commitment: NoKy Keyboard operates completely offline. We do not collect, monitor, store, log, or transmit any text you type, passwords you enter, or any personal data whatsoever.

1. Information Collection & Use

NoKy Keyboard is designed as a standalone, zero-tracking utility tool. Because our application functions entirely locally on your mobile device:

  • No Keystroke Logging: We never log, track, or record your keystrokes, messages, credentials, or text fields.
  • No Personal Data Collection: We do not ask for, collect, or store personal identifiable information (PII) such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or device identifiers.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: The application does not contain any hidden telemetry, analytics frameworks, or user behavior trackers.

2. Device Permissions & Why We Need Them

To deliver advanced customization and layout control features, NoKy Keyboard requests specific Android system permissions. These permissions are utilized exclusively to run features locally on your device:

  • Draw Over Other Apps (System Alert Window / Display Pop-up): This permission is required solely to operate the Live Bubble overlay and quick notification features. It allows you to invoke or toggle the keyboard layout globally over any screen.
  • Receive Boot Completed (Run at Startup): This enables the optional Auto-Start functionality, ensuring the keyboard service is registered and immediately available to use when your device restarts.
  • Vibrate Control: This permission is used strictly to handle the haptic feedback engine, letting you fine-tune the vibration intensity of keypresses.
  • Audio Settings: This is used to play local keypress sound effects based on your desired volume configuration.

3. Third-Party Services & Links

NoKy Keyboard is 100% free and completely ad-free. We do not bundle any third-party advertising SDKs or data-monetization networks.

If you choose to voluntarily support the project via any external project donation links, those financial transactions are processed securely and externally by third-party payment gateways (such as your browser processing a donation page). No banking, credit card, or financial details are ever handled, seen, or stored by the NoKy Keyboard application itself.

4. Children's Privacy

Our application is safe for users of all ages. Because we do not collect, capture, or transmit any data from any user device, we do not knowingly or unknowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13, in strict compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR guidelines.
